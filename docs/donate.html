Every penny counts!

Control Alt Delete helps domestic violence victims flee abusive situations with donations and sponsorships from people like you. Your donation can be included in your Federal Tax Deductions under section 501(c)(3) of the IRS tax code, in addition to the Arizona Charitable Tax Credit!

Control Alt Delete is a Qualifying Charitable Organization (QCO) for the Arizona Charitable Tax Credit. For 2020, the maximum Arizona QCO credit is $400 for single and head of household taxpayers and $800 for married taxpayers filing a joint return.  Control Alt Delete LLC QCO Code: 22198

We help domestic violence victims flee abusive relationships. With support from our community and people like you, we provide emergency cab rides, hotel accommodations, home security, lock change assistance, meals, storage, and assistance with moving expenses. Our average cost per person for an escape is $12.28.
